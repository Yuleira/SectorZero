Support

We're here to help. Browse common topics or reach out directly.

Common Topics

  • Account Issues Problems signing in, resetting your password, or managing your account? Make sure you're using the same sign-in method (Apple, Google, or Email) as when you first registered. If your account was deleted or you're locked out, contact us below.
  • Location Permissions SectorZero requires location access to enable territory claiming and exploration. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → SectorZero and set it to While Using the App. Make sure precise location is enabled.
  • Communication Features The Comms tab allows you to communicate with nearby survivors. Range depends on your equipment tier. If messages aren't sending, check your internet connection and ensure your callsign is set in your profile.
  • Gameplay Questions Questions about territory claiming, building structures, resource scavenging, or the trading marketplace? Tap the Manual option in Settings to replay the onboarding guide, which covers all core mechanics in detail.
