Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

SectorZero is a location-based interactive experience developed by Yuleira. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data. By using SectorZero, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal & Account Data

We collect the following information when you create and use an account:

  • Email address (for authentication and account management)
  • Display name (Callsign)
  • Profile picture (if voluntarily provided)
  • Device identifiers for session security and fraud prevention

2.2 Location Data (Core Functionality)

SectorZero is a location-based experience. We collect:

  • Precise GPS location while the app is actively in use
  • Movement coordinates (path history) to render territory zones and gameplay metrics

Location data is:

  • Collected only while the app is in use
  • Never accessed in the background without explicit user permission
  • Used strictly to enable gameplay mechanics and maintain system integrity

2.3 Gameplay & Financial Data

We collect:

  • Survival logs and territory metrics
  • Exploration distance and gameplay statistics
  • Resource inventory levels
  • In-app purchase transaction records (processed securely via Apple StoreKit)

We do not store full payment card information.

2.4 Diagnostic & Technical Data

To maintain stability and performance, we may collect:

  • Anonymous crash reports
  • Performance logs
  • Server interaction metrics

These logs do not directly identify individual users.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA / GDPR)

If you are located within the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity – to provide the game service
  • User consent – for location tracking
  • Legitimate interest – for security, fraud prevention, and service optimization

You may withdraw consent at any time by disabling permissions or deleting your account.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Enable core gameplay mechanics
  • Verify location-based actions and maintain gameplay integrity
  • Enable proximity-based communication features (e.g., PTT)
  • Maintain leaderboards and progression systems
  • Improve server performance and stability

If voice communication features are enabled, voice data is transmitted in real-time and is not recorded or stored on our servers.

5. Data Storage & International Transfers

User data is stored on secure servers located within the European Economic Area (EEA). If data is transferred outside the EEA, appropriate safeguards are implemented in accordance with GDPR requirements. All reasonable technical and organizational security measures are implemented to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

  • Active accounts: Data is retained while the account remains active.
  • Deleted accounts: All associated personal data is permanently erased within 30 days, except where retention is legally required (e.g., financial transaction records).

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with essential service providers:

  • Supabase – Secure cloud database and authentication services
    supabase.com/privacy
  • Apple (StoreKit) – In-app purchase processing
    apple.com/legal/privacy

These providers process data solely to support app functionality.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Request export of your account data
  • Withdraw consent

You may delete your account at any time via: Settings → Delete Account
Account deletion is irreversible. For additional requests, contact us via email.

9. Children's Privacy

SectorZero is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. The latest version will always be available through our official channels.
